Grilled Steak

Description:

A juicy, flavorful grilled steak seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic butter.
simple recipe is easy to prepare and makes a delicious main course.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ribeye or sirloin steak
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary (optional)

Steps:

  1. Remove the steak from the refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking.
  2. Pat the steak dry with paper towels.
  3. Rub both sides with olive oil, then season generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat a grill or skillet over high heat until very hot.
  5. Cook the steak for 3–4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or longer to your preferred doneness.
  6. During the last minute of cooking, add the butter, garlic, and rosemary.
  7. Spoon the melted butter over the steak for extra flavor.
  8. Remove the steak from the heat and let it rest for 5–10 minutes before serving.
